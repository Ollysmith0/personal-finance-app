# Privacy Policy for Personal Finance App

**Last Updated**: November 13, 2025

## Introduction

Personal Finance ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Storage
- **All data is stored locally** on your device using AsyncStorage
- **We do NOT collect any personal information**
- **We do NOT transmit any data** to external servers
- **We do NOT track your usage** or behavior

### Data We Store Locally
The app stores the following information on your device only:
- Transaction records (amount, date, category, description)
- Budget settings
- Reminder preferences

This data:
- ✅ Remains on your device
- ✅ Is under your control
- ✅ Is NOT backed up to our servers
- ✅ Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

We do NOT use any third-party services including:
- Analytics tools
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- Cloud storage providers

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Transfer your data to other services
- Access your data remotely

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted content.

## Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your device's built-in security protects your data
- We recommend using device passcode/Face ID
- Data is only accessible through the app
- No transmission means no interception risk

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: View all data within the app
- **Delete**: Uninstall the app to remove all data
- **Export**: Currently not supported (future feature)

## Data Retention

- Data persists as long as the app is installed
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data
- No data is retained after uninstallation

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Notifying within the app (for significant changes)
